## Public resume sharing

Share your resume via a unique link directly with recruiters, without them needing to create an account. Enable sharing from the button at the top of the editor.

## View counter

Know how many times your public resume has been viewed. The counter appears in the sharing panel and on your resume's public page.

## Mobile-friendly resume page

Your resume's public page now automatically adapts to phones and tablets thanks to smart zoom.
